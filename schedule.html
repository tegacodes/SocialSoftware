2 - Jan 29: Social Spaces

  • What is a social space? We will consider historic and contemporary social spaces and their characteristics. What are the differences between public urban space, online bulletin boards, platforms like geocities, friendstar, myspace, facebook and ello? What do we mean by social capital, reputation based economies and ambient intimacy?
    • What have been some approaches of the artist and designer working to explore these spaces?
